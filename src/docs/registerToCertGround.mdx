# How To Register To CertGround&trade;

1. Go to the following url: https://ibew120.certground.com
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and on the right click the green register button.
3. You will be taken to the registration initiation page.
4. Here you will enter the information needed to verify your identity with CertGround™
5. Please enter an username. For example mine is natac13
6. Enter the email address you have on file with The Hall.
7. Then enter a password of at least 8 characters long, upper and lower case letters, with at least one number.
8. Then re-enter that password to confirm.
9. Next you will have to select the checkbox to verify that you have read and agree with the End User License Agreement and Privacy Policy. You can view those documents with the links provided.
10. Then click the register button.
11. There will be a notification in the top left of the screen to let you know if this action was successful.
    - Blue background: with a text saying to check your email to complete the registration process.
    - Red background: there was an error, try again and follow any instructions the error message may give you. If the error persists please email Sean Paul Campbell.
12. When you receive the successful notification, please check your email that you used to register.
13. The email will come from the following address "Training Coordinator &lt;noreply@training.ibew120.certground.com&gt;"
14. You should see your Halls letter head at the top.
15. Please read the email and select the link "Complete Registration To CertGround™".
16. The link will re-direct you to the [login page](https://ibew120.certground.com/login) if and when successful.
17. Please enter your email address and password to login to CertGround™.
18. You will be taken to the <a href="/docs-member-profile">Member Profile</a> page.
