A **Proof of Training** print out can be obtained by using the QRCode Share feature. When you generate a QRcode you will see "GO TO SHARE LINK" button. This will take you to a page which has you organization's letter head and the Course Training Records you wished to share. At the bottom there is a button which allows you to print off **Proof of Training**.

This is a great option for those who do not have a cell data sevices plan or will be in an area with bad cell service for there Job Safety Orienation.
